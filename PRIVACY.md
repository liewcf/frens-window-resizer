# Privacy Policy for Frens Window Resizer

**Last Updated: December 19, 2025**

Frens Window Resizer is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

## 1. Data Collection
Frens Window Resizer **does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII)**. We do not track your browsing history, your location, or any other personal data.

## 2. Local Storage
The extension uses `chrome.storage.local` solely to store the following technical information on your device:
*   **Window Bounds:** The previous width, height, and position of your browser window (used to restore the window size when you toggle the resizer off).
*   **Selected Preset:** Your preferred resize target (e.g., "Mobile" or "Tablet").

This data is stored strictly on your local machine and is never uploaded to any external servers or shared with third parties.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not share any data with third parties. No data is sold, traded, or otherwise transferred to outside parties.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at **liewcf@gmail.com** or open an issue on our project repository.
